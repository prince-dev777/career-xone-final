Privacy Policy

Transparency and Trust are the core values of Career Xone.

Welcome to Career Xone. We value the trust you place in us and recognize the importance of secure transactions and information privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how Career Xone collects, uses, shares or otherwise processes your personal information through Career Xone website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and educational background when you:

  • Register for a course or test series.
  • Fill out an enquiry form on our website.
  • Subscribe to our newsletters or updates.
  • Interact with us via social media or customer support.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide you with academic counseling and course details.
  • Process your admission and fee payments.
  • Send important updates regarding class schedules, tests, and results.
  • Improve our website and educational services based on feedback.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored on secure servers and is accessible only to authorized personnel.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Career Xone does not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners and trusted affiliates for the purposes outlined above.

5. Student Results & Photos

By enrolling in Career Xone, you agree that we may use your name, photograph, and academic results for promotional purposes in our brochures, website, and social media channels to celebrate your success.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
